What Are Microgreens?

Microgreens are the young and tender seedlings of edible vegetables and flower greens that are harvested just after the first leaves (cotyledon leaves) have developed. They are sometimes referred to as vegetable confetti!!

Nutritional Value of Microgreens

Microgreens are a superfood, packed with nutrients, minerals and antioxidants.  Don’t let their small size deceive you, as their nutrient content is concentrated and can provide up to nine more times the nutritional value than their more mature varieties. Microgreens will generally only retain their full nutritional potential for several days after harvesting, after which time these nutritional values start to wane and are less beneficial, even if their taste may linger longer. To this end, it is essential that microgreens are eaten as soon as possible after harvesting. Vitamins in microgreens

Cooking and Recipes Using Microgreens

Microgreens are flavorful and can easily be incorporated into your diet to give you a boost of flavor and nutrients! You can use microgreens in salads, smoothies, juiced, and serves as a great substitute for lettuce on sandwiches and wraps. Microgreens may be eaten raw, and adds color and flavor into a variety of cold and warm dishes.
